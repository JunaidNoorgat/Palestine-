Our Story

Witnessing the suffering in Palestine moved us to action. Our hearts break seeing the daily struggles of Palestinians who are denied basic human rights - from food insecurity to lack of shelter.

We've created these elegant necklaces as a way to raise funds that will be sent through verified non-profit organizations to provide humanitarian aid to Palestine.

The necklace features beautiful Arabic calligraphy engraved on mirror acrylic Perspex, with a 50cm adjustable chain and 5.5cm x 1cm pendant. Each piece is a symbol of hope and solidarity.
